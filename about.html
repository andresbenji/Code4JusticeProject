About the XY Mental Health Foundation


The problem we are facing in our Code 4 Justice project is dealing with the stigma men face when dealing with mental health awareness. This is a real issue that has a great impact on a large percentage of men today, with nearly a third of men reporting having experienced a period of depression at some point in their life. Men also account for 3.5 times the number of suicides as women, this goes to show the impact our issue has today. Our goal is to create a non-profit organization and utilize a website we will create together to bring awareness to this issue. We hypothesize by providing awareness and resources with our site we can make an impact on these numbers, we can track our success
